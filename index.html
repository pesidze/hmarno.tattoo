ABOUT

I'm Emma — a tattoo artist based in Barcelona, working by appointment only from a private studio in the city centre. My work lives at the intersection of nature and graphic art. I draw from the world around us — botanicals, birds, insects, organic forms — and translate them into clean black lines, delicate detail, and quiet precision. The style I work in is somewhere between sketch and graphic: structured, but never rigid. Expressive, but always considered.

I spent years building my practice in Berlin, where I learned that a tattoo is not just an image — it's a conversation between two people, and a piece of art that will be carried for a lifetime. That's why I work slowly, carefully, and only on designs that feel right. Every piece I make is created exclusively for you. I don't repeat designs. Ever.

Booking with me starts with a conversation. I want to understand not just what you want, but why — what moves you, what you love, what you want to carry on your skin. From there, I design something that couldn't belong to anyone else.

My studio is a safe space for everyone. Queer-friendly, judgment-free, and built on the belief that everyone deserves to feel comfortable in the chair.

If you're looking for a custom tattoo in Barcelona — something considered, personal, and made to last — I'd love to hear from you.
